Stressors

-- Percent of colonies affected by stressors anytime during the quarter. 
	A colony may be affected by multiple stressors during the quarter

1/ Tracheal mites, nosema, hive beetle, wax moths, etc.

2/ Includes American and European foulbrood, chalkbrood, stonebrood, paralysis
	 (acute and chronic), kashmir, deformed wing, sacbrood, 
	  IAPV, Lake Sinai II, etc

3/ Includes weather, starvation, insufficient forage, 
	queen failure, hive damage/destroyed, etc.

4/ Alaska, Delaware, Nevada, New Hampshire, and Rhode Island not published separately 
	to avoid disclosing data for individual operations.
